Life simulation written in Rust. Source here: .

Cells are allowed to evolve through mutation and natural selection.
Cells have a simple genome giving them unique characteristics:

  • Green allows them to collect energy from the sun
  • Red allows them to eat other cells
  • Dark blue allows them to defend
  • Light blue allows them to move around

A special trait, intelligence (denoted by a square), allows them to move towards or away from the nearest edible biot instead of randomly, depending on if the prey/attacker is stronger.
All non-green traits cost energy.
